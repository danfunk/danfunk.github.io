Project Description

All too often we get trapped in an unhealthy spiral, attacking the same problem in the same way over and over again. Sometimes we need an outside influence, a fresh external examination of the problem that provides a new way of seeing and discussing the issues.
What we Did?
A company was struggling over proposed solutions to deal with an online gaming platform. Invitations to play a game were not received in a timely fashion. This issue was impacting everyone from content development to infrastructure and IT. No two people saw the problem in the same way. For this reason solutions were disjoint rather than collaborative. This diagram helped cement a fresh approach on the issue and allowed tighter partnership between business development, data anyalytics, software development and system administration.
Presentation
Pitching this fresh idea took time and effort within the company. This diagram grew and adjusted over time, evolving based on feedback from a broad spectrum of concerned individuals. As departments saw the diagram adjust to address their specific concerns, support for the idea grew. This eventually spurred a massive overhaul of the invitation system.
