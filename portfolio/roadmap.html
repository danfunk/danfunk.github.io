Project Description

Successful software development is not an isolated exercise. Coordination between teams of software developers, content developers, data scientists, quality control, and customer support is absolutely critical. Diagrams like these help communicate an agile software teams long term objectives in a way that helps other managers anticipate periods of change and high activity.
What we Did?
We developed this diagram for an organization that was driven by software development, but which spanned a large number of other disciplines. It was critical to communicate a sense of cadence in the development process, in order to give other teams a predictable work cycle.
Benefits?
Establishing a visual connection between major deliverables and the cadence of the agile software cycle allowed managers from other teams to walk away with confidence in the predictability of this team. This greatly enhanced trust between the teams and helped develop a much healthier working relationship.
